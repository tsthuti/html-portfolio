The Best Movies According to Tanisha

My Top 3 movies of all-time

Tangled

I used to watch this movie a lot when I was a kid. Rapunzel is my favourite Disney princess.

Madagascar 2: Escape to Africa

This movie holds a special place in my heart because Alex, the main character, finally gets to meet his family and experience life outside of a zoo.

Despicable Me

A classic. Watching this movie I woke up was a part of my daily routine at the age of 7. I could watch it forever and ever.
